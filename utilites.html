Voltage Divider

Voltage dividers are used to divide voltage. And for some dumb reason people use them for power supply 'regulators' lol. Nope. Thats dumb. But here's a calculator.

$$V_{out}=\frac{R_2}{R_1+R_2} \cdot {V_{in}}$$ $$I_{waste}=\frac{V_{in}}{R_1+R_2}$$ $$P_{1_{noload}}={R_1} \cdot {I_{waste}}^{2}$$ $$P_{2_{noload}}={R_2} \cdot {I_{waste}}^{2}$$
